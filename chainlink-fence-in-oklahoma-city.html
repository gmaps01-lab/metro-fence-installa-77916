Frequently Asked Questions About Chainlink Fence

How much does it typically cost to install a chainlink fence in Oklahoma City?
The cost to install a chainlink fence in Oklahoma City varies depending on the length, height, and any custom features like privacy slats or gates. On average, most residential installations range from $15 to $27 per linear foot. We provide free on-site estimates to give you the most accurate quote based on your property’s layout and your specific needs.
How long does the chainlink fence installation process take from start to finish?
For most residential projects in OKC, the installation process takes 1-3 days once we begin work, depending on the size and complexity of the job. After your initial estimate, scheduling typically occurs within 1-2 weeks, but this can vary during busy seasons or after heavy rains, which are common here. We’ll always keep you updated on our timeline and notify you of any weather-related delays.
Is Metro Fence Installation Company licensed and insured to work in Oklahoma City?
Yes, Metro Fence Installation Company is fully licensed and insured to operate in Oklahoma City and throughout Oklahoma. We comply with all local regulations and permitting requirements, so you can have peace of mind knowing your project is being handled professionally and in accordance with city codes.
What is the process for getting a chainlink fence installed on my property?
Our process starts with a free on-site consultation where we assess your property, discuss your needs, and provide a detailed quote. Once you approve the estimate, we handle any necessary city permits for you. Before installation, we’ll mark property lines (often with your input) and call OKIE 811 to locate underground utilities—which is required by law. On installation day, we set the posts in concrete, allow them to cure, and then attach the chainlink fabric and any gates or additional features. We do a final walkthrough with you to ensure everything meets your expectations.
Do you offer any guarantees or warranties on your chainlink fence installations?
Absolutely. We stand by our workmanship with a one-year labor warranty, and the chainlink materials we use typically come with a manufacturer’s warranty of 10-15 years against rust or defects. If you experience any issues with your fence within the warranty period, just give us a call and we’ll address them promptly.
