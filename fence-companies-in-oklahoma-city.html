Frequently Asked Questions About Fence Companies

How much does it typically cost to install a new fence in Oklahoma City with Metro Fence Installation Company?
The cost of a new fence installation in Oklahoma City depends on several factors, such as the length and height of the fence, the materials selected (wood, chain link, vinyl, etc.), and any customizations like gates or decorative features. For example, basic chain link fences generally start around $18–$25 per linear foot installed, while wood or vinyl fences are typically $30–$45 per linear foot. We offer free on-site estimates so we can assess your property and provide a detailed, transparent quote based on your specific needs and local permitting requirements.
How long does the fence installation process usually take in the OKC area?
For most standard residential projects in Oklahoma City, the installation process takes about 2 to 4 days once materials are delivered and any necessary permits are secured. Smaller fences may be completed faster, while larger or more complex projects (such as those requiring removal of an old fence, custom gates, or challenging terrain) may take up to a week. We always discuss timelines with you during the estimate and keep you updated if weather or other local factors affect the schedule.
