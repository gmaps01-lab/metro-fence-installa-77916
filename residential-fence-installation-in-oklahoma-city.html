Frequently Asked Questions About Residential Fence Installation

How much does residential fence installation typically cost in Oklahoma City?
The cost of residential fence installation in Oklahoma City varies depending on the fence material, height, and total length. For example, a standard 6-foot wood privacy fence usually ranges from $25 to $40 per linear foot, including materials and labor. Chain link fences are more affordable, while ornamental iron or vinyl options are higher. We provide free, on-site estimates to give you an accurate quote tailored to your property and preferences.
How long does it take to install a new fence at my home in OKC?
Most residential fence installations in Oklahoma City are completed within 2 to 5 days, depending on the size of your yard, fence type, and weather conditions.
